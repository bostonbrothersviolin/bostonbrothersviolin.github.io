About Us

Two brothers.
One language.

Boston Brothers are Tejas and Pranav Majunath — siblings who grew up sharing a music stand and a passion for pushing what two violins can do together. Rooted in Indian Classical Carnatic training and drawn to the energy of contemporary performance, they blend technique with emotion in a way that feels both timeless and alive. From intimate concert halls to festival stages, their performances are known for their intensity, chemistry, and surprising range.
