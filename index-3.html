About Us

Two brothers.
One language.

Boston Brothers are [Name] and [Name] — siblings who grew up sharing a music stand and a passion for pushing what two violins can do together. Rooted in classical training and drawn to the energy of contemporary performance, they blend technique with emotion in a way that feels both timeless and alive.

From intimate concert halls to festival stages, their performances are known for their intensity, chemistry, and surprising range. Born and raised in Boston, the city's creative spirit runs through everything they play.
